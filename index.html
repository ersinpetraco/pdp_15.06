ACME is Petra's everyday single origin — not a fixed blend, but a rotating South American lot chosen each season for the same reason every time: richness, balance, drinkability. What stays constant is the profile — a rich chocolatey body, smooth balanced acidity and a lingering sweetness. What changes is where it comes from. Whether you're pulling espresso or brewing a morning V60, ACME is designed to be your reliable everyday companion.

Don't be afraid of the fruity notes — after all, coffee is a fruit too.
